# Lab 3 Quickstart: Design Review at a Glance

Five things to know before the lab starts.

---

**1. The full two hours is team work.**
No individual technical exercises today. Sit with your team from minute zero.

**2. You already have the raw material.**
Your Lab 2 six-section proposal, your Lab 1 reflection, your scaffold, and your vision call are all inputs. You do not start from scratch — you sharpen, extend, and add what is missing.

**3. What is missing from your Lab 2 proposal.**
The proposal covered: problem, solution, architecture (prose), timeline, roles, and IRB checklist. The Design Review additionally requires: measurable success criteria, an actual architecture diagram, a data flow trace, safety threats, fallback UX description, and data governance. All built today using the guides and templates in `design-review/`.

**4. The team contract must be signed and committed before you leave.**
Syllabus requirement — due Week 3. Template: `design-review/templates/team-contract-template.md`. Commit it as `TEAM-CONTRACT.md` in your repo root.

**5. Due: Thursday 2 April at 23:59.**
Submission = team repo (matching tree in README) + Google Form (link shared in lab and on Teams).
