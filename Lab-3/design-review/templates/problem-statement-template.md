# Problem Statement: Final Form

Use this to consolidate and sharpen the problem statement from your Lab 2 proposal into the precise form required by the Design Review.

---

## The Formula

**"[Specific user group] struggle to [specific action] because [root cause], which means [concrete consequence]."**

---

## Working Through It

**From your Lab 2 proposal, what did you write for the problem?**

```
[paste your Lab 2 problem statement]
```

**Sharpen: who exactly?**
Replace any generic group ("students", "small businesses", "people") with a specific description.

```
Instead of: "students"
Write: "first-year business students at Georgian universities who are learning financial reporting"
```

**Sharpen: what exactly can they not do?**
Replace any activity with the specific thing they cannot do efficiently.

```
Instead of: "manage expenses"
Write: "reconcile handwritten receipts with their digital accounting spreadsheets"
```

**Sharpen: why exactly?**
Name the root cause — the thing that makes the problem exist.

```
Instead of: "it is difficult"
Write: "because they cannot type Georgian text quickly enough to match OCR-parsed fields"
```

**Sharpen: what is the consequence?**
Name the cost — in time, money, friction, or missed opportunity.

```
Instead of: "which wastes time"
Write: "which costs them 40+ minutes per week of manual data re-entry"
```
